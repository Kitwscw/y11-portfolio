    • Aesthetics
      • aesthetics is important because a good looking main character in a video game is what draws people to the game & is usually what people think of first when thinking about the game
      • I will ensure it looks good by asking peoples opinions & gatheing a range of different viewpoints on whether or not it is visually appealing & adjust it until i am happy with the result
    • Functionality
      • Functionality is important because if the character isnt able to move at all then there isnt much reason to use it
      • i will ensure it is able to move, jump, walk, & run without visual issues at least by testing if the movement works correctly by moving each limb & joint around & checking if anything unexpected happens
    • Usability
      • Usability is important because if you can't use it then there isn't much reason to have it because well, its useless
      • i will achieve this by asking people to test it & see if they can use it for various things
